Presentation of Colombia

Cristian Jiménez
Description

It is a country in South America.
It is the twenty-eighth most populous country in the world.
The name Colombia comes from the surname of the fifteenth-century Italian explorer Christopher Columbus.

Geography

It has a total area of 1,141,749 km²
It has borders with Brazil, Ecuador, Peru, Venezuela and Panama.
The country has about 270 partially uninhabited islands in the open sea.
Colombia has a tropical climate.
Colombia is made up of 32 departments and a capital district.
It is the only country in South America with coasts on the Atlantic and Pacific oceans.

Curiosities

1) - Colombia is one of the most biodiverse countries in the world.
2) - Colombia is the largest exporter of emeralds.
3) - A study places it among the eight countries responsible for half of the destruction of biodiversity in the world.
4) - Due to its location, Colombia does not have climatic seasons. 5) - Colombia is a country of intermediate size, despite this it has approximately between 10% or 20% of plant species worldwide.
